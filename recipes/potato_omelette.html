Spanish potato omelette

Description

Spanish Potato Omelette, known as Tortilla Española , is one of the most traditional dishes in Spanish cuisine. It is simple, made with just a few ingredients, but rich in flavor. It can be served hot or cold

Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 1 medium onion (optional, but traditional in many regions)
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup olive oil (for frying)
  • Salt to taste

instructions

  1. Prepare the potatoes

    2. Peel the potatoes and slice them thinly (about 3 mm thick). Slice the onion thinly as well.

  2. Cook the potatoes and onion

    3. Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan over medium heat. Add the potatoes and onion. Cook slowly for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the potatoes are soft but not crispy.

  3. Drain the oil

    4. Remove the potatoes and onion from the pan and drain excess oil. Set aside.

  4. Beat the eggs

    5. In a large bowl, beat the eggs and add salt. Mix in the cooked potatoes and onion. Let the mixture rest for 5 minutes.

  5. Cook the omelette

    6. Add a small amount of oil back to the pan and pour in the mixture. Cook over medium-low heat for about 5 minutes, until the bottom is set.

  6. Flip the omelette

    7. Place a large plate over the pan and carefully flip the omelette onto the plate. Slide it back into the pan to cook the other side for another 3-5 minutes.

  7. Serve

    8. Let it rest for a few minutes before slicing. Serve warm or at room temperature.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with fresh bread.
  • Pair with a green salad.
  • Cut into small squares for tapas.

Spanish potato omelette

Description

Spanish Potato Omelette, known as Tortilla Española, is one of the most traditional dishes in Spanish cuisine. It is simple, made with just a few ingredients, but rich in flavor. It can be served hot or cold.

Ingredients (4 servings)

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 1 medium onion (optional, but traditional in many regions)
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup olive oil (for frying)
  • Salt to taste
Start with care and follow each step.

Instructions

  1. Prepare the potatoes

    Peel the potatoes and slice them thinly (about 3 mm thick). Slice the onion thinly as well.

  2. Cook the potatoes and onion

    Heat the olive oil in a large frying pan over medium heat. Add the potatoes and onion. Cook slowly for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the potatoes are soft but not crispy.

  3. Drain the oil

    Remove the potatoes and onion from the pan and drain excess oil. Set aside.

  4. Beat the eggs

    In a large bowl, beat the eggs and add salt. Mix in the cooked potatoes and onion. Let the mixture rest for 5 minutes.

  5. Cook the omelette

    Add a small amount of oil back to the pan and pour in the mixture. Cook over medium-low heat for about 5 minutes, until the bottom is set.

  6. Flip the omelette

    Place a large plate over the pan and carefully flip the omelette onto the plate. Slide it back into the pan to cook the other side for another 3-5 minutes.

  7. Serve

    Let it rest for a few minutes before slicing. Serve warm or at room temperature.
Time to plate and serve with style.

Serving Suggestions

  • Serve with fresh bread.
  • Pair with a green salad.
  • Cut into small squares for tapas.
